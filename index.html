Alone | Puzzle & survival (Class Project)

Gameplay Programmer

Alone is a Survival / Puzzle game where players must survive in a hazardous environment. To escape, they need to explore various areas to find passwords required to unlock the safe room. Adding to the tension, a monster roams the facility, constantly posing a threat and making survival a true challenge.

To The Trip | Racing (Bit Egg Game Jam 2025)

Gameplay Programmer & Art

In this high-speed driving game, players must skillfully navigate through traffic and pass through Turbo gates that enable the use of Turbo boosts, while avoiding obstacles and escaping the relentless pursuing fog. Precision driving and quick reflexes are key to surviving and achieving high scores.
